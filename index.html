Projects

TOWER

TOWER is an Android app that I developed with three other Hofstra students, and it is the first mobile app that I have ever worked on. The goal was simple: develop a better way for students to get rid of their used books.


With the help of the Google Books API, users could upload their used books to our app while Google would assist in providing the cover image and book descriptions.


The name “TOWER” comes from the huge stack of used textbooks that we were stuck with at the end of a semester.
